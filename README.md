# Monte Cristo Project Outline

This repository contains a project outline with Markdown files in preparation for testing my upcoming project management software.

The content focuses on Alexandre Dumas' "The Count of Monte Cristo," particularly the revenge arc after Edmond Dantès discovers the treasure on the island of Monte Cristo.

## Contents

This repository contains detailed markdown documentation about the revenge arc:

- **revenge-overview.md** - Overview of the Count's revenge mission
- **revenge-fernand-mondego.md** - Revenge against Fernand (the jealous rival)
- **revenge-danglars.md** - Revenge against Danglars (the greedy banker)
- **revenge-villefort.md** - Revenge against Villefort (the ambitious prosecutor)
- **characters.md** - Profiles of all major characters
- **timeline.md** - Complete chronology from 1815-1844
- **themes.md** - Analysis of moral and philosophical themes
- **treasure.md** - Details about the treasure of Monte Cristo

## The Story Arc

After 14 years of wrongful imprisonment, Edmond Dantès escapes and discovers a vast treasure. He transforms himself into the Count of Monte Cristo and systematically destroys the three men who betrayed him, while also helping those who were kind to him.
